# Brand — Maxi's Mighty Mindgames

_Status: active_

## Visual direction

Playful space-adventure learning for children aged 5–11. Interfaces use dark cosmic surfaces, bright high-contrast energy accents, generous rounded shapes, and friendly character art.

## Palette

- Space dark: `#0f0f23`
- Space mid: `#1a1a3e`
- Success / primary action: `#22c55e`
- Energy pink: `#e94560`
- Reward gold: `#fbbf24`
- Comet cyan: `#67e8f9`
- Comet purple: `#a78bfa`
- Primary text: `#ffffff`
- Secondary text: `#a1a1aa`

Colors are implemented as tokens in the native `AppTheme`; screens should not introduce one-off color literals.

## Typography

Use the system rounded design throughout. Titles are bold or heavy; body copy stays regular and concise. Never use tiny instructional text for child-facing actions.

## Voice

Warm, specific, encouraging, and never punitive. Give one correction at a time and tell the child what to do next. Prefer “Begin on the green star” over generic errors.

## Interaction

- iOS is the primary platform.
- Touch targets are at least 44×44 points.
- Essential instructions never rely on colour alone.
- Optional motion respects Reduce Motion.
- The established pulsing Circuit Challenge hex animation is a protected product detail and must remain unchanged.
