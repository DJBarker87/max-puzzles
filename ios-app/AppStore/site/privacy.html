Privacy Policy

This policy covers the Maxi's Mighty Mindgames mobile app for iPhone and iPad.

The short version

The app does not send personal data to the developer, show advertising or track users. It works without a developer account. When Apple iCloud is available, private family profiles and a small progress summary sync between devices using the same Apple Account.

Information stored on the device

To provide its learning features, the app saves the following information locally on the device:

  • Optional child profile names and hand preference
  • Scores, rewards, progress and formation mastery
  • Writing traces used for grown-up progress review
  • App preferences, including sound and writing settings
  • Custom practice words added by a grown-up
  • Optional pronunciation recordings made by a grown-up

Detailed writing paths and attempts, custom practice words and optional pronunciation recordings stay on the device and are not included in iCloud progress sync.

Private progress sync through Apple iCloud

When the device is signed in to iCloud, the app saves this small data set through Apple's private key-value storage:

  • Child profile name, creation date and hand preference
  • Completed Dot-to-Dot pictures and the selected tap-or-trace style
  • Completed Comet Writer symbols, best formation scores and the last practised symbol
  • Circuit Challenge completion, stars, best times, attempts and reward totals

Apple makes this value available only to copies of the app using the same Apple Account. Dominic Barker does not operate a server for this feature and cannot view or retrieve the value. It is not used for advertising, analytics or tracking. Apple's handling of iCloud is governed by the Apple Account holder's agreement with Apple.

The learning games continue to work offline. Changes sync again when iCloud becomes available.

Microphone access

The microphone is optional. It is requested only when a grown-up passes the in-app adult gate and chooses to record a pronunciation for a custom practice word. The recording stays on the device and can be removed with the custom word.

Exports and sharing

A grown-up may explicitly create a progress report or printable worksheet and open Apple's system share sheet. Nothing is shared unless the grown-up chooses a destination. Once exported, the selected destination's own privacy terms apply to that copy.

What the app does not contain

  • No developer account or separate sign-in
  • No adverts or behavioural advertising
  • No analytics, tracking SDKs or third-party app SDKs
  • No in-app purchases
  • No chat, social feed or unrestricted web browser
  • No location, camera or contacts access

Children's privacy

The app is designed for children and does not transmit information to or retain information for the developer. Private Apple iCloud sync is used only to continue the same family's progress across its devices. Grown-up tools—including detailed reports, custom words, profile deletion and microphone recording—are protected by an adult-level multiplication question.

Retention and deletion

Local information remains on the device until it is deleted in the app or the app is removed. Removing only the app does not necessarily remove its private iCloud progress value. The adult-gated Clear All Data action clears local learning data and places a reset marker in iCloud so an older synced device cannot restore the cleared progress. Profile deletion is also shared with synced devices.

Security

Local app data is protected by the security controls of iOS or iPadOS and the device owner. Synced progress uses Apple's private iCloud service. The developer does not maintain a remote database containing children's learning records.

Changes to this policy

This page may be updated when the app changes. The effective date above will identify the current version. The App Store privacy answers will also be reviewed before each release.
