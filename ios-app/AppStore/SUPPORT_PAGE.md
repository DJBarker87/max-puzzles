# Maxi's Mighty Mindgames Support

Maxi's Mighty Mindgames is a handwriting, number-recognition, spelling and mathematics app for iPhone and iPad. No developer account is required, and every game works offline.

## Common questions

### How do I practise one letter?

Open Comet Writer and choose Quick Practice. Pick lowercase, capitals or numbers, then tap the symbol you want.

Comet Writer works with a finger. A capacitive stylus or Apple Pencil is optional on compatible devices.

### How does Dot-to-Dot Discovery work?

Open Dot-to-Dot Discovery and choose one of two styles: Tap dots asks the child to press the next number, while Trace lines asks them to draw from the previous dot to the next. The next number is shown and spoken, and every visible dot belongs to the trail. Varied spacing encourages the child to recognise the number rather than simply choose the nearest dot.

The main ranges are 1–10, 1–15 and 1–20, with selected 1–25 challenges. The gallery contains 84 illustrated puzzles. After completing a trail, the picture expands into a full-screen colouring stage with colours chosen for that specific image. Children can tap a region to shade it or colour directly with a finger or Apple Pencil. A separate 1–5 Star Spot activity and occasional optional number-writing lesson in Comet Writer remain optional.

### How does Star Speller work?

Choose a Year 1 practice group and a 3, 5 or 10-word mission. The app speaks the hidden word in a short context sentence, the child types it, then practises its letter formation. Hint use and errors are saved to the active child profile so adaptive practice can revisit words that need help. Grown-ups can add their own list and optional example sentences in My Words.

### How do family profiles work?

Tap the profile avatar on the main menu to add or switch children. After more than one profile exists, the app asks who is playing when it opens. Each child keeps separate progress.

### Does progress sync between devices?

Yes. When iCloud is available, profile details and a compact progress summary sync privately between devices using the same Apple Account. Detailed writing trails, custom words and voice recordings remain on the device. The games still work offline and resume syncing later.

### How do I turn writing lines on or change letter size?

Open a Comet Writer lesson and choose Tools. Use the writing-lines toggle and letter-size slider.

### How does Apple Pencil-only mode work?

On iPad, open Tools during a writing activity and enable Pencil only. The writing pad then accepts Apple Pencil input and ignores finger drawing. Fingers still work for buttons and navigation. This option is hidden on iPhone.

### Why is the microphone requested?

Microphone access is optional and used only when a grown-up records a pronunciation for a custom practice word. Open Grown-up tools, pass the adult check, open My Words and choose Record. Audio remains on the device.

### How do I choose times tables?

Open Circuit Challenge, choose Quick Play, enable Custom Settings, then enable multiplication or division. Select each table you want to include.

### How do I remove saved information?

Open the app settings and choose Clear All Data. This adult-gated action clears learning data on the device and tells other devices using the same Apple Account not to restore older progress. A grown-up can also delete individual profiles, custom words and recordings from the adult-gated tools.

### Sound or speech is not playing

Check the in-app sound, voice and music controls. The app respects the device's audio environment and does not override other audio aggressively.

## Privacy

The app has no adverts, developer account, tracking or third-party analytics. A small profile and progress summary can sync privately through Apple iCloud. Detailed writing trails, custom words and optional recordings stay on the device unless a grown-up explicitly exports a report through the system share sheet. Read the published privacy policy at `https://maxis-mighty-mindgames-support.vercel.app/privacy`.
