## Common questions

### How do I practise one letter?

Open Comet Writer and choose Quick Practice. Pick lowercase, capitals or numbers, then tap the symbol you want.

### How do I turn writing lines on or change letter size?

Open a Comet Writer lesson and choose Tools. Use the writing-lines toggle and letter-size slider.

### How does Apple Pencil-only mode work?

On iPad, open Tools during a writing activity and enable Pencil only. The writing pad then accepts Apple Pencil input and ignores finger drawing. Fingers still work for buttons and navigation. This option is hidden on iPhone.

### Why is the microphone requested?

Microphone access is optional and used only when a grown-up records a pronunciation for a custom practice word. Open Grown-up tools, pass the adult check, open My Words and choose Record. Audio remains on the device.

### How do I choose times tables?

Open Circuit Challenge, choose Quick Play, enable Custom Settings, then enable multiplication or division. Select each table you want to include.

### How do I remove saved information?

Open the app settings and choose Clear All Data. A grown-up can also delete individual Comet Writer profiles, custom words and recordings from the adult-gated tools.

### Sound or speech is not playing

Check the in-app sound, voice and music controls. The app respects the device's audio environment and does not override other audio aggressively.

## Privacy

The app has no adverts, account, tracking or third-party analytics. Learning records and optional recordings are stored on the device unless a grown-up explicitly exports a report through the system share sheet. Read the published privacy policy at `https://maxis-mighty-mindgames-support.vercel.app/privacy`.
