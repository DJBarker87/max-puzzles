# Privacy Policy for Maxi's Mighty Mindgames

Effective date: 14 July 2026

Maxi's Mighty Mindgames is a children's handwriting and mathematics app. It is designed to work without an account or internet connection and does not send personal data to the developer or to third parties.

## Information used on the device

The app may store the following information locally on the device so that its learning features work:

- child profile names entered by a grown-up;
- left- or right-handed preference;
- game progress, scores and rewards;
- sampled writing paths and formation feedback;
- app settings;
- custom practice words entered by a grown-up; and
- optional pronunciation recordings made by a grown-up.

This information is processed and stored only on the device. The app does not upload it to a developer server, use it for advertising or sell it.

## Microphone

The microphone is optional. It is available only inside the adult-gated My Words feature so that a grown-up can record a pronunciation for a custom practice word. The app asks for microphone permission only when the grown-up chooses to record. Recordings remain on the device and can be deleted in the app.

## Sharing

A grown-up may choose to create a progress report or puzzle and share it using Apple's system share sheet. Sharing is initiated by the grown-up. The privacy practices of any destination they choose, such as Mail or Files, are governed by that destination. Maxi's Mighty Mindgames does not automatically transmit those files.

## Analytics, advertising and tracking

The app contains no advertising, tracking technology or third-party analytics SDK. It does not use an advertising identifier and does not track people across apps or websites.

## Retention and deletion

Local information remains on the device until it is deleted in the app, the relevant profile or recording is removed, all app data is reset, or the app is uninstalled. The app's settings include a Clear All Data action.

## Children's privacy

The app does not provide accounts, social networking, chat, unrestricted web access or public sharing. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children because no information is transmitted to or retained by the developer.

## Changes

If the app's data practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated before the changed version is released.
