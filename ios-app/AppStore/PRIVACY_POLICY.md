# Privacy Policy for Maxi's Mighty Mindgames

Effective date: 17 July 2026

Maxi's Mighty Mindgames is a children's handwriting, number-recognition, spelling and mathematics app. It works without a developer account and does not send personal data to the developer, advertisers or analytics companies. When Apple iCloud is available, it uses Apple's private key-value storage to keep family profiles and a small progress summary available on devices using the same Apple Account.

## Information used on the device

The app stores the following information locally on the device so that its learning features work:

- child profile names entered by a grown-up;
- left- or right-handed preference;
- game progress, scores and rewards;
- sampled writing paths and formation feedback;
- app settings;
- custom practice words entered by a grown-up; and
- optional pronunciation recordings made by a grown-up.

Detailed writing paths and attempts, custom practice words and optional pronunciation recordings stay on the device and are not included in iCloud progress sync.

## Private progress sync through Apple iCloud

When the device is signed in to iCloud, the app saves the following small data set through Apple's `NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore`:

- child profile name, creation date and hand preference;
- completed Dot-to-Dot pictures and the selected tap-or-trace style;
- completed Comet Writer symbols, best formation scores and the last practised symbol; and
- Circuit Challenge completion, stars, best times, attempts and reward totals.

Apple makes this value available only to copies of the app using the same Apple Account. Dominic Barker does not operate a server for this feature and cannot view or retrieve the value. The app does not use it for advertising, analytics or tracking. Apple's own handling of iCloud is governed by the Apple Account holder's agreement with Apple.

The learning games continue to work offline. Changes sync again when iCloud becomes available.

## Microphone

The microphone is optional. It is available only inside the adult-gated My Words feature so that a grown-up can record a pronunciation for a custom practice word. The app asks for microphone permission only when the grown-up chooses to record. Recordings remain on the device and can be deleted in the app.

## Sharing

A grown-up may choose to create a progress report or puzzle and share it using Apple's system share sheet. Sharing is initiated by the grown-up. The privacy practices of any destination they choose, such as Mail or Files, are governed by that destination. Maxi's Mighty Mindgames does not automatically transmit those files.

## Analytics, advertising and tracking

The app contains no advertising, tracking technology or third-party analytics SDK. It does not use an advertising identifier and does not track people across apps or websites.

## Retention and deletion

Local information remains on the device until it is deleted in the app, the relevant profile or recording is removed, all app data is reset, or the app is uninstalled. Removing only the app does not necessarily remove a private iCloud progress value. The adult-gated Clear All Data action clears local learning data and places a reset marker in iCloud so that an older synced device cannot restore the cleared progress. Profile deletion is also shared with synced devices.

## Children's privacy

The app does not provide developer accounts, social networking, chat, unrestricted web access or public sharing. Information is not transmitted to or retained by the developer. Private Apple iCloud sync is used only to continue the same family's progress across its devices.

## Changes

If the app's data practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated before the changed version is released.
