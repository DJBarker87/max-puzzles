## Description

Turn handwriting and maths practice into a space adventure.

Maxi's Mighty Mindgames brings together two playful learning experiences for children aged 6–8: Comet Writer and Circuit Challenge. Short missions, calm feedback and meaningful rewards help learners keep going without turning every mistake into a noisy interruption.

COMET WRITER

Practise all 26 lowercase letters, 26 capitals and the numbers 0–9. Every symbol has a clear starting point, stroke order and direction, with writing-line guidance for ascenders, x-height, baseline and descenders.

• Follow the glow, connect the stars, then write independently
• Get immediate feedback on starting point, direction, path and line placement
• See a formation score out of 100 and earn Comet Points for every attempt
• Pick any symbol instantly in Quick Practice
• Recall chosen letters from sight or sound
• Hear phonics and write the matching letter in Sound Mission
• Write a whole common word on one continuous writing line
• Build control first with Flight School's lines, curves, slants and loops
• Move from the screen to real pencil-and-paper practice
• Choose finger input or Apple Pencil-only input on compatible iPad models
• Adjust letter size and turn writing lines on or off

GROWN-UP MISSION CONTROL

Create separate local profiles, choose left- or right-handed writing, see formation mastery, replay saved writing trails and make a local PDF progress report. Grown-ups can also add custom practice words and optionally record a pronunciation on the device.

CIRCUIT CHALLENGE

Solve the glowing hex, then follow the connector carrying its answer. Play a gentle adventure or customise a quick game with addition, subtraction, multiplication and division. Choose the exact times tables to practise, change the number range and grid size, or create printable puzzles for offline play.

BUILT FOR FOCUSED PRACTICE

• No adverts
• No in-app purchases
• No account or sign-in
• No third-party analytics or tracking
• Learning records and optional recordings are stored on the device
• Works without an internet connection

A grown-up gate protects progress tools, profiles, reports and optional microphone recording. The app does not automatically upload a child's writing, name, scores or voice recording; a grown-up can explicitly export a local report through the system share sheet.

## App Review notes

No account, sign-in, payment or network connection is required. The app contains no adverts, in-app purchases, third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Suggested review path:

1. On the hub, select Comet Writer.
2. Select Quick Practice to choose any lowercase letter, capital or number.
3. Select Start with c to see the three-stage guided formation lesson.
4. Under Ready for a challenge, select Word Mission to write a complete word on one screen.
5. Under Grown-up tools, open Mission Control or My Words. The random multiplication question is the parental gate; there is no fixed passcode.
6. Microphone access is requested only after a grown-up passes the gate, opens My Words and explicitly chooses to record a local pronunciation.
7. Return to the hub, select Circuit Challenge, then Quick Play and Custom Settings. Enable multiplication or division to see the individual times-table picker.

All child profiles, scores, writing traces, custom words and optional audio recordings are stored locally. A grown-up may explicitly export a locally generated progress report or puzzle through the system share sheet. Pencil-only input is shown only on iPad, where Apple Pencil is available.
